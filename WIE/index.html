IEEE APSIT WIE

IEEE WIE is one of the world’s leaders in changing the face of engineering. Our global network connects nearly 20,000 members in over 100 countries to advance women in technology at all points in their life and career. IEEE WIE members make lifelong friendships, acquire influential mentors, and make a difference for the benefit of humanity.

About WIE

The goal of Women In Engineering is to bridge this glaring divide and encourage women to take up more leadership positions. We aim to create a more inclusive atmosphere, especially in the field of engineering. Webinars, seminars and various other interactive activities are organised regularly, apart from the conferences and magazines arranged by IEEE. These webinars and conferences also double as excellent platforms to forge new connections in the corporate world. With active members and a dedicated executive committee, we hope to change the face of engineering!

WOMEN IN LEAD

IEEE APSIT SB WIE AG, in association with the IEEE BOMBAY SECTION WIE and the WIE AGs of VJTI, Sandip Foundation and Universal College, presents "Women In Lead", a highly educational three-day webinar series by an all-women guest panel.

  • Day 1: Seema Kawale on "WIE Scholarships and Awards" • Tharani K on "Benefits of Volunteering"
  • Day 2: Ajaita Shah on "Business Opportunities in Rural Areas" • Arti Gupta on "Women Entrepreneurship"
  • Day 3: Dipika Trehan on "Career Development" • Neha Tripathi on "How LinkedIn Profile Benefits Our Career"
