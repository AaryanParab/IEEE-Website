About

The IEEE Computer Society sponsors workshops and conferences, publishes a variety of peer-reviewed literature, operates technical committees, and develops IEEE computing standards. It supports more than 200 chapters worldwide and participates in educational activities at all levels of the profession, including distance learning, accreditation of higher education programs in computer science, and professional certification in software engineering.

Inauguration

IEEE APSIT Computer Society was inaugurated on 7th March 2020 in an intimate ceremony which saw the presence of all IEEE APSIT student members, faculty mentors as well as a few non-members. As a token of celebration, a cake was cut by Ms Megha Ben, an IEEE APSIT CS YP, Dr Uttam Kolekar, principal of A P Shah Institute of Technology and branch councellor for IEEE APSIT and Prof. Kiran Deshpande, faculty mentor for IEEE APSIT CS.

Events

CODERS WEEK



The Coders Week' was a week-long programming endeavour,starting from 1st June 2020. The entire event was divided into two major phases - the Learning Phase, which lasted for 6 days and the Project Phase, which was from the 7th to 10th. The participants could learn about the three major domains - Machine Learning, BlockChain and Android App Development. Registrations were accepted for any or all three events. The top 10 projects selected by eminent faculty were reviewed by a panel of IEEE CS YPs. This panel picked out the best 3 from each domain and mentored them in their respective domain. We received over 600 registrations and a total of 83 participants received participation certificate, while 61 received completion certificate. Fahmi Jabbar, Rahul Sunil and Vritika Naik were declared the winners for ML, BlockChain and Android App Development domains, respectively.
